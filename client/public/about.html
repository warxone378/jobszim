Our Mission

Jobszim is Zimbabwe's premier job board, dedicated to connecting talented job seekers with employers across the nation. We believe that finding the right job should be simple, transparent, and accessible to everyone.

What We Do

We provide a platform where employers can post job openings (subject to admin approval to ensure quality) and job seekers can discover opportunities, apply directly, and build their careers. Our features include job alerts, company profiles, real‑time chat between applicants and employers, and detailed analytics for recruiters.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We verify every job posting to maintain trust.
  • Inclusivity: We welcome job seekers from all backgrounds.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our platform based on user feedback.

Meet the Founder

Jobszim was created by Warzone, a passionate developer who wanted to build a tool that genuinely helps people find meaningful work. The entire platform was built from scratch using Python, Flask, and a lot of dedication – all on a mobile phone!
